Green Transport Policy Evaluator

A tool for evaluating the impact of transportation policies on mode choice and promoting sustainable transportation.

About the Project

This project aims to provide a comprehensive evaluation of various transportation policies and their impact on urban mobility. By simulating different scenarios, users can understand the potential benefits and drawbacks of each policy.

Project Goals

  • Promote sustainable transportation options
  • Reduce traffic congestion and emissions
  • Enhance public transport usage
  • Provide data-driven insights for policymakers

How It Works

Users can input various policy parameters such as bus time reduction, parking fee increase, and bus frequency. The tool then calculates the projected mode share, average travel time, and cost, as well as the reduction in car trips and emissions.
